背景：用星体模拟符号学视域下的认同机制。     1.亚里士多德：圈层说。      认为存在一个不变的自我认同之根，即太阳（capital）绕着地球（root）转，越中间的是越切身的、越直接的、越与生俱来的。  

  2.哥白尼：日心说↣rootless      以惰性的、原始朴浊的capital为中心，而观察者由静态走向动态，诗意地栖居在大地上。      但由于未解构掉capital的中心性而丧失了行动力。       

 3.布努诺：无限宇宙和多重世界的学说↣centerless      认为世界是无限的，宇宙是去中心化的（任何事物都可以成为中心），事物间的围绕运动是暂时的。      但是预设了绝对的时空观念，把capital视作一个背景性的秩序，其把符号学中心视作不断地同质化、不断地吸附能指的存在，最后把一切意义都耗散掉（此处模拟了热寂理论）。        

4.爱因斯坦：广义相对论。      认为没有旋转运动发生，循环不存在，无条件的中心化运动不存在，背景性的符号学空间不存在。      因此，没有一个实体化的capital，只是符号使用频繁的地方形成了一个引力式，扭曲了群体的语用学习惯，造成了语用场的坍缩，然后以最大速率促进了其他符号学机制和意志性的分配。也就是说，该理论认为事件仍在走直线，只是被人类扭曲的符号学机制偏折了。

Article 1. Concept of indigenous people of Ukraine

1. 1. Indigenous people have right to self-determination as a part of Ukraine, establish the political status within the Constitution and the laws of Ukraine, freely perform the economic, social and cultural development.

2. 3. Indigenous people have the right according to the Constitution and the laws of Ukraine to keep and strengthen the special political, legal, social and cultural institutions, keeping at the same time the participation in economic, social and cultural life.

1. 3) forced assimilation or forced integration in any form;

4) encouragement or incitement of the racial, ethnic or religious hatred directed against them.

5. Indigenous people of Ukraine determine own national symbolics and procedure for its application with compliance with laws of Ukraine.

6. Indigenous people have the right to revive, use, develop and transfer to future generations the history, language, traditions, writing and literature, to recover and keep objects of material and non-material cultural heritage.

7. Denial of ethnic origin or ethnic identity of indigenous people of Ukraine is forbidden.

Article 4. Cultural rights of indigenous people of Ukraine

2. 3.
